ClojureScript Koans

The ClojureScript koans walk you along the path of enlightenment to learning the ClojureScript language. You will explore the mysteries of ClojureScript through a series of interactive exercises in your web browser.

Those unfamiliar with Clojure and ClojureScript may find them quite different from other programming languages. The koans will expose you to the fundamentals of ClojureScript, from basic concepts and syntax through more advanced techniques, in a friendly and supportive manner.
